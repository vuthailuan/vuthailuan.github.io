About

I am currently a tenured Associate Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics, Texas Tech University (TTU).

Prior to this appointment, during 2019–2024 I served as an Assistant Professor and Associate Professor in the Math & Statistics department, and as a member of the Center for Computational Sciences (CCS) at Mississippi State University (MSU).

During 2014–2019, I held Visiting Assistant Professor positions in the Applied Math Department, University of California, Merced (UCM), and in the Department of Mathematics, Southern Methodist University (SMU), Dallas, TX.

I received my PhD degree in 2014 from the Department of Mathematics, University of Innsbruck (UIBK), Austria.
